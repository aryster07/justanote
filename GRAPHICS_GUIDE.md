## üöÄ How to Use

### Using ChatGPT (DALL-E)

1. **Open ChatGPT** with DALL-E access
2. **Choose a prompt** from `CHATGPT_GRAPHICS_PROMPTS.md`
3. **Copy and paste** the entire prompt
4. **Review the generated image**
5. **Request modifications** if needed:
   - "Make the gold color more vibrant"
   - "Add more sparkles"
   - "Change the text to say..."
   - "Create a variation with different layout"
6. **Download** the final version in high resolution

### Using Other AI Tools (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, etc.)

1. **Read** `PROJECT_BRIEF.md` for context
2. **Adapt the prompts** from `CHATGPT_GRAPHICS_PROMPTS.md` to your tool's format
3. **Include** the brand colors and style keywords:
   - Gold gradient: #D4AF37 to #C5A059
   - Minimalist, elegant, romantic
   - Serif font for headings (Playfair Display style)
   - Clean, modern design

### Working with Human Designers

1. **Share** `PROJECT_BRIEF.md` with your designer
2. **Reference** specific prompts from `CHATGPT_GRAPHICS_PROMPTS.md` for ideas
3. **Ensure** they follow the brand colors and style guidelines
4. **Request** variations and iterations as needed
